Our services

Cleaning services tailored to your space.

From everyday home cleaning to full handover cleans, we tailor each visit around the property, the client and the standard required.

Residential Cleaning

For busy households that need a consistent, reliable clean with attention to detail.

  • Kitchen surfaces, sinks, appliances and cupboard fronts
  • Bathrooms, mirrors, shower screens and sanitaryware
  • Dusting, vacuuming, mopping and general room resets
  • Regular, one off or tailored cleaning visits

Airbnb Cleaning

Guest ready cleaning for Airbnb hosts and short term rentals between bookings.

  • Turnover cleaning between guests
  • Bathroom and kitchen refreshes
  • Bed presentation and final visual checks
  • Reporting obvious issues noticed during the clean

End of Tenancy Cleaning

A more detailed clean for tenants, landlords and property managers before handover.

  • Inside cupboards and accessible surfaces
  • Kitchen degreasing and bathroom descaling
  • Floor cleaning, skirting boards and general dust removal
  • Ideal before check out, inspection or new tenancy

Deep Cleaning

A thorough reset for homes needing extra time, care and attention.

  • Detailed cleaning of high use rooms
  • Extra focus on build up, dust and hard to reach areas
  • Great for seasonal resets or after busy periods
  • Suitable before hosting, moving or returning home

Office Cleaning

Professional cleaning for small offices, studios and workspaces.

  • Desk areas, shared surfaces and meeting spaces
  • Kitchenette and washroom cleaning
  • Bins, floors and touchpoint cleaning
  • Flexible visits around your working hours

Holiday Let Cleaning

Cleaning and presentation support for short stays, serviced accommodation and guest lets.

  • Guest focused presentation
  • Fresh, tidy and welcoming room resets
  • Attention to kitchens, bathrooms and living spaces
  • Designed for repeat bookings and reliable turnovers

Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Do you offer regular cleaning?

Yes. We offer regular residential cleaning depending on availability, including weekly and fortnightly options.

Do you clean Airbnb properties?

Yes. We clean Airbnb and short term rental properties between guests, with a focus on bathrooms, kitchens, bedrooms and presentation.

Do you offer office cleaning?

Yes. We clean small offices, studios and workspaces, including desks, shared areas, kitchenettes, washrooms and floors.

What is included in a deep clean?

A deep clean gives more time and attention to build up, dust, surfaces, kitchens, bathrooms, floors and areas that may not be covered in a standard clean.

Do you do end of tenancy cleaning?

Yes. We provide end of tenancy cleaning for tenants, landlords and property managers preparing a property for inspection or handover.

How do I get a quote?

You can call, email or WhatsApp us with the property location, number of rooms, type of clean and your preferred date.
