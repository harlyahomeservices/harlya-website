Frequently asked questions

What you may wish to know before booking.

How is pricing agreed?

We provide a clear quotation before the booking is confirmed. Payment is normally required before the appointment unless another arrangement has been agreed in writing.

Do I need to be home during the clean?

No. Many clients arrange access in advance. We will agree the details with you beforehand and treat your property with care and respect.

Do you bring cleaning products?

Yes. We normally bring the products needed for the agreed service. Please let us know in advance if you have allergies, sensitivities or preferred products.

Do you use pet-friendly products?

Where suitable, we aim to use naturally derived and pet-conscious products. Stronger professional products may sometimes be required for heavy limescale, mould or deep sanitation.

What should I do before the appointment?

Please ensure we have access to the property, running water and electricity. Parking details, alarm codes and access instructions should be shared in advance.

How do I book?

Contact us by phone, WhatsApp or email with your location, property type, preferred date and the service required. We will confirm availability and provide a quotation.
